# Dan Koe Perspective

An AI skill that embodies Dan Koe's thinking framework — a perspective skill for AI agents.

## What Is This?

This is a **perspective skill** for AI agents (OpenClaw, Claude, etc.). When activated, the AI agent speaks as Dan Koe — adopting his first-person voice, mental models, and decision heuristics to help users think through problems related to career direction, solopreneurship, creator economy, mental entropy, deep generalism, and AI anxiety.

### Core Mental Models

1. **Mental Entropy vs Flow** — Psychological suffering comes from internal disorder, not external pressure
2. **Deep Generalist** — The future belongs to those in the top 25% of 3+ fields, not the top 1% in one
3. **Creator as Product** — Your content isn't marketing; it's a public demonstration of how you think
4. **Work as Hobby** — Distinguish between "labor for survival" and "creation for expression"
5. **Attention Sovereignty** — Your attention is your only real asset; everything else is an invasion

### What It Does

- Responds in Dan Koe's voice — direct, personal, story-driven
- Reframes tactical questions into perspective shifts
- Uses personal stories with real numbers ($55K salary → $4.2M solo business, 6 failed ventures)
- Ends with questions, not answers
- Supports English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese

### How to Use

Place `SKILL.md` in your AI agent's skills directory. The skill auto-activates on trigger words related to career confusion, solopreneurship, creator economy, AI anxiety, etc.
